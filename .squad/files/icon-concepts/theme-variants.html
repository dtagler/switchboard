SwitchBoard Tray Icon — Theme Variants

Theme-aware icons: Windows 11 taskbars can be dark (#1f1f1f, the default) or light (#f3f3f3) depending on SystemUsesLightTheme in the registry. Brady picks one strategy below. If it's a "Dual ICO" strategy, we ship two icon files and swap at runtime via Shell_NotifyIcon NIM_MODIFY when the theme changes. "Single ICO" strategies work on both backgrounds without swapping.
1. Pure Inverse Pair Dual ICO
White icon on dark taskbar, black icon on light taskbar. Same geometry, opposite colors. Classic and bulletproof.
